# TrustCredit AI — Demo Video Script (2 Minutes)

## Setup
- Open the live demo: https://ecliptic-magnetar.vercel.app
- Have a browser with MetaMask or any wallet extension ready
- Screen recording tool: OBS, Loom, or built-in (Win+G on Windows)

---

## Script

### [0:00 - 0:15] HOOK — The Problem
**Narration:**
> "1.7 billion people worldwide cannot access loans — not because they're untrustworthy, but because they have no credit history. Traditional banks reject them."

**Screen:** Show a simple text slide or the landing page hero text.

---

### [0:15 - 0:30] SOLUTION
**Narration:**
> "TrustCredit AI solves this by using artificial intelligence and blockchain to create a decentralized credit scoring system built on Creditcoin."

**Screen:** Show the landing page — scroll to feature cards (AI Scoring, On-Chain Reputation, DeFi Lending, Global Access).

---

### [0:30 - 0:45] CONNECT WALLET
**Narration:**
> "Users simply connect their wallet. Our AI engine analyzes their on-chain activity — transactions, DeFi participation, staking, and peer endorsements."

**Screen:** Click "Connect Wallet" → show RainbowKit modal → connect wallet.

---

### [0:45 - 1:10] DASHBOARD & SCORE
**Narration:**
> "Instantly, TrustCredit AI generates a Trust Score from 0 to 1000. Here we can see a score of 636 — Grade C — with a detailed breakdown: wallet activity, repayment history, staking balance, and reputation. The AI also provides personalized recommendations to improve your score."

**Screen:** Navigate to `/dashboard` → show the animated score gauge → scroll to score breakdown with progress bars → show AI recommendations.

---

### [1:10 - 1:30] APPLY FOR A LOAN
**Narration:**
> "With a qualifying trust score, users can apply for a loan. Move the slider to choose your amount and duration. The smart contract automatically calculates the interest rate based on your score — higher scores get better rates, from 3% to 12%."

**Screen:** Navigate to `/apply-loan` → drag amount slider → drag duration slider → show the real-time summary → show rate tiers panel → click "Submit Loan Request" → show approval success.

---

### [1:30 - 1:45] LENDER VIEW
**Narration:**
> "Lenders can deposit liquidity, review borrower risk profiles, and track their portfolio — all transparently on the blockchain."

**Screen:** Navigate to `/lender-dashboard` → show stats cards → scroll to loan portfolio table.

---

### [1:45 - 2:00] CLOSING
**Narration:**
> "TrustCredit AI enables fair, transparent, global access to credit — powered by AI and secured by Creditcoin smart contracts. No bank account needed, just your wallet. This is the future of financial inclusion."

**Screen:** Show landing page CTA section with "Get Started Now" button.

---

## Recording Tips
1. **Resolution:** 1920x1080 (1080p)
2. **Audio:** Clear narration, no background noise
3. **Speed:** Don't rush — let the UI animations play
4. **Upload to:** YouTube (unlisted) or Loom, then share the link
5. **Length:** Keep strictly under 2 minutes
