01 — The Problem

1.7 Billion People Are Financially Excluded

Traditional credit systems are centralized, biased, and exclude billions worldwide.

❌ Who Gets Excluded?

  • Freelancers & gig workers with no pay stubs
  • Small farmers & micro-businesses
  • People in developing countries
  • Immigrants with no local credit history
  • Anyone without a bank account

❌ Why Current Systems Fail

  • Credit bureaus are centralized & opaque
  • Scoring algorithms are biased
  • No interoperability across borders
  • Data is siloed and insecure
  • Users don't own their credit data
02 — The Solution

TrustCredit AI — Decentralized Trust-Based Lending

We combine AI credit scoring with Creditcoin smart contracts to create a transparent, borderless lending system.

Global Access

04 — AI Credit Scoring Engine

Trust Score Formula

📊 Wallet Activity — 30%

Transaction count, volume, wallet age, unique interactions

✅ Repayment History — 40%

Loans repaid, on-time rate, default history, amounts

🎯 Staking & DeFi — 20%

Staking balance, DeFi positions, liquidity provided, yield

⭐ Reputation — 10%

Peer attestations, DAO memberships, social verification

05 — Smart Contract

TrustLoan.sol — On-Chain Lending

Solidity smart contract deployed on Creditcoin EVM managing the full loan lifecycle.

Profile Management

  • createProfile()
  • updateTrustScore()
  • addAttestation()

Lending

  • depositLiquidity()
  • requestLoan()
  • approveLoan()

Settlement

  • repayLoan()
  • liquidateLoan()
  • withdrawLiquidity()

Key Features

  • Score-gated access (min 400 to apply)
  • Tiered interest rates based on trust score
  • Automatic score reduction on default (-100 pts)
  • On-chain reputation attestation system
  • Lender liquidity pool management
  • Full event logging for transparency
